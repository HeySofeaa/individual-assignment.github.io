I love playing video game!

One of my favourite video game is ace attorney. It is game about lawyers defending their client by investigating the case, talking to witnesses, and presenting evidence in courtroom. Other than that, it also has a great animation, story, worlbuilding and background music.

The gameplay involves investigating crime scenes, gathering evidence, and engaging in courtroom battles to prove the innocence of their clients. The games are known for their engaging storylines, colorful characters, and dramatic "Objection!" moments where players present evidence to contradict witnesses' testimonies. Since its debut in 2001, the franchise has gained a dedicated fanbase and expanded into various media, including anime, manga, and stage plays.

Other than that, my favourite video game is a lso a rhythym video game because I enjoy music. Especially J-pop. The game combines story elements, where players interact with characters and follow their personal and professional growth, with rhythm-based performances where players tap along to music tracks. Ensemble Stars is celebrated for its extensive character roster, engaging storylines, and vibrant music, appealing to fans of the idol genre.

In the rhythm game portion, players participate in live performances where they tap on-screen prompts in time with the music to score points. Successful performances help idols improve their skills and unlock new content. Additionally, players can collect and upgrade character cards, each with unique attributes and artwork, to enhance their idol unit's performance and progress in the game.

Making video edits!

In my free time, I love to channel my creativity by making edits of my current hyperfixations. These can range from video games to my favorite musicians and anime. Each project allows me to immerse myself deeply in the subject matter, whether it's capturing the dynamic action of a game, highlighting the talent and personality of a beloved artist, or bringing to life the vibrant world of an anime series. Through editing, I can express my passion and appreciation for these interests in a unique and personalized way.

Creating these edits involves a lot of detailed work, from selecting the perfect clips and music to fine-tuning transitions and effects. It's a rewarding process that not only enhances my technical skills but also deepens my connection to the things I love. Sharing these edits with others who share my interests adds another layer of enjoyment, as it opens up opportunities for feedback, collaboration, and community building. Ultimately, this hobby is a perfect blend of creativity and fandom, making my leisure time both productive and enjoyable.
