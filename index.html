A little bit about me!

I grew up in the energetic metropolis of Petaling Jaya , which is found in Malaysia's state of Selangor. I have loved the color pink since I was a small child because it makes me feel happy and comfortable. My favorite animals are cats because of their loving and lively personalities, which constantly make me smile.

Another one of my favorite activities is reading novels because I enjoy losing myself in other worlds and following the adventures of different people. Whether I'm taking on challenging missions or partaking in exciting action, I find playing video games to be highly entertaining and captivating. I also like to dress up and use fashion to reflect who I am. I like to try out new looks and styles to show off my uniqueness. My favourite fashion style that I always go crazy for is lolita fashion.
