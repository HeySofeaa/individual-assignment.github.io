This page is dedicated to my family who I will forever love and cherish.
Now let's start!

Father

First, let's start with my father. My father's name is Mohd Razee Bin Ramly. He was born on 6 August 1982. A fun fact about his birthday is that my father and I share the same birthday! Every time I tell someone about this, they get a bit shocked that we share the same birthday. And to be honest, I find their reaction very cute. We even likes the same food, which is fried chicken! Like father like daughter am I right?

My father works as a clerk in an insurance company called Liberty Insurance . It's a company where people get insurance for their vehicles and other sorts. He is very good at his job and dedicated to it. As far as I know, he has been working in that company for almost 20 years. He started working there when I was still a kid.

At home, he is a very dedicated husband and father who always caters to our needs. He always helps around the house by assisting my mother in preparing food, repairing the house if needed, and helping my little sister with her homework.

Mother

Next is my lovely and beautiful mother who always works hard everyday for the family. Her name is Noraliza Binti Mior Abdul Malek. She was born in November 1981, just 1 year older than my father. She is a dedicated housewife who cherished her family very much.

My mother is an excellent cook. Since I was a kid, I always believed that she had a superpower in making any food delicious. Even though the food looks simple, but it taste super delicious. I guess nothing beats a mothers cooking right? One of my favourite dish made by my mom is buttermilk prawn. Yum!

She loves visiting places like shopping malls and trying new trendy restaurant. Whenever we go outing together, she would buy food that are unique and trendy. What a ffun hobby! She wears glasses just like me. I have noticed that the women from my mother side mostly wears glasses. the same goes for me and my younger sister. It runs in the blood I guess.

Younger sister

Younger brother

This big boy is my younger brother. His name is Mohd Rasydan Bin Mohd Razee. He was born in 2010 and is now 14 years old. He was diagnosed with autisme when he was years old. To our surprise, he indeed does have autisme. From that day onwards, he received therapy and has been going to a special class for disable people.

However, having an autisme does not stop him from being a smart kid. In school, he got good grade. He is in highschool now and is a class representative which is very impresive. In school, he attend many activities such as swimming, gardening, bowling, sports and mmore! He is also very reliable at home by helping washing his own dishes, cleanig his room, and arranging his laundry. I hope he become a great person when he grows up.
